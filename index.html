What is FRP?

Fibre Reinforced Polymer – The Future of Infrastructure Materials

🔬 Advanced Tech

Revolutionary Composite Material

FRP (Fibre Reinforced Polymer) is a high-performance composite material made by combining strong glass fibers with a polymer resin matrix. This creates a material that's incredibly strong yet remarkably lightweight.

Unlike traditional materials like cast iron or concrete, FRP doesn't corrode, rust, or degrade over time. It maintains its structural integrity even in the harshest environments – from chemical exposure to extreme weather conditions.
